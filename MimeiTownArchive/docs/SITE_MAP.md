## Planned Sites

### 1. 未明町役場

Role: official announcements, public records, ceremony pages.
Visual: civic, simple header, old municipal colors, dense information.
Clues: date inconsistencies, missing PDF numbers, public notice IDs.

### 2. 未明町観光協会

Role: harmless travel content hiding route and landmark clues.
Visual: soft local tourism site, photos, seasonal banners.
Clues: old festival name, closed walking route, altered lighthouse copy.

### 3. 未明日報

Role: local news archive.
Visual: newspaper layout, article columns, archive search.
Clues: disappearance article, correction notice, reporter initials.

### 4. 県立未明高校 同窓会

Role: school history and alumni posts.
Visual: reserved school page, announcements, scanned newsletters.
Clues: class roster, club activity room, sudden event cancellation.

### 5. 未明町立図書館 OPAC

Role: catalog search and local history index.
Visual: utilitarian library search.
Clues: call numbers, missing local-history book, librarian notes.

### 6. 深夜ラジオ「灯台波」

Role: transcript archive and audio-like text logs.
Visual: small independent radio archive.
Clues: caller names, broadcast timestamps, phrase repetition.

### 7. 港湾気象ログ

Role: weather and tide records.
Visual: government-adjacent data tables.
Clues: impossible visibility reading, wind direction, fog warning.

### 8. 未明交通 バス時刻表

Role: movement timeline validation.
Visual: old transport company page, tables, route maps.
Clues: last bus time, holiday route, closed stop.

### 9. 個人ブログ「海の灯りを見た日」

Role: emotional witness account.
Visual: 2000s blog, small photos, monthly archive.
Clues: image filenames, comments, edited post dates.

### 10. 廃墟写真サイト

Role: visual evidence and location matching.
Visual: photography portfolio, subdued gallery.
Clues: hidden EXIF-like captions, building labels, recurring red mark.

### 11. 掲示板ログ保管庫

Role: rumor layer and false leads.
Visual: archived forum, monospaced logs, thread IDs.
Clues: tripcodes, banned words, one poster with accurate details.

### 12. 郷土資料館 閉鎖ページ

Role: central historical reveal.
Visual: abandoned official site frozen after closure.
Clues: exhibition names, folklore, map fragment.

### 13. 町議会議事録検索

Role: motive and cover-up layer.
Visual: record search with agenda PDFs represented as HTML pages.
Clues: budget line, deleted committee name, speaker order.

### 14. 慰霊碑保存会

Role: late-game ritual and names.
Visual: small civic volunteer site.
Clues: stone inscription, annual date, family names.

### 15. Hidden Site: 明けない灯台

Role: final puzzle site.
Visual: sparse, archival, not supernatural UI.
Clues: final code assembly, ending branch.

### 16. Investigation Notebook

Role: player tool, answer checks, progress.
Visual: not a game dashboard; more like inherited notes.
Clues: structured prompts, unlocked pages, hints.

## Independence Rules

- Do not add one shared global navigation across all sites.
- Each site must have its own typography, spacing, header treatment, and content rhythm.
- Shared footer text should be avoided unless story-justified.
- The player should discover sites by keywords, links, records, and the notebook.
